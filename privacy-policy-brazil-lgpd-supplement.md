# Brazil — LGPD Supplement (English) — Airlock

**Last updated:** May 2026  

This supplement is for **data subjects located in Brazil** under **Lei nº 13.709/2018 (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados Pessoais — LGPD)**. Read it together with the [main Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md).

If this supplement **conflicts** with the main Policy **for Brazil only**, this supplement **controls** for processing subject to Brazilian law.

## Legal bases (bases legais) — Article 7 LGPD

Depending on the processing activity, we rely on appropriate bases under **Art. 7**, including:

- **I** — consent, when required and obtained;  
- **V** — execution of contracts or preliminary procedures to which you are a party, at your request;  
- **IX** — legitimate interests, with balance against your fundamental rights;  
- **X** — credit protection (generally not applicable to this product);  
- **VI** — routine exercise of rights in judicial, administrative, or arbitration proceedings (if ever relevant).

For purely **local** application data that **we never receive**, practical exercise of rights (access, correction, anonymization, deletion, portability) occurs through **your control** of device files and app features.

## International transfer of data (LGPD Chapter V)

The main Policy states that **we** do not, for this reference distribution, operate a mandatory cloud pipeline receiving your workspace data. If **you** send requests to servers **outside Brazil**, international transfer rules may apply to **that** recipient under LGPD and the other jurisdiction’s law.

If we later transfer personal data from Brazil to other countries as **controller**, we will document appropriate safeguards and mechanisms required by LGPD (e.g. SCCs, adequacy, contractual clauses) in an updated notice.

## Rights of data subjects (LGPD Articles 18–19)

Subject to LGPD exceptions, you may request, as applicable:

- Confirmation of processing;  
- Access;  
- Correction;  
- Anonymization, blocking, or erasure;  
- Portability;  
- Information about sharing;  
- Revocation of consent (where processing is based on consent);  
- Review of decisions based solely on automated processing affecting your interests.

Submit requests via the **Contact** section of the [main Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md), or directly: **mferreira95@protonmail.com** (email) and **https://github.com/MrChase95/airlock** (support website / issues).
